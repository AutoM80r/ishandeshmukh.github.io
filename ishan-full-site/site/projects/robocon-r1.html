Robocon R1 Ball Launcher

Manual robot for DD Robocon 2025 (Theme: Harvest Season). Holonomic base, multi-crop seedling picker, and flywheel launcher.

STM32F103C8RPi 4BROS2Robocon 2025AIR 22
overview

R1 is the manual operated robot for DD Robocon 2025. The theme was "Harvest Season" — robots needed to collect seedlings from a field and launch balls into targets. R1 handles the manual driving and launching phase, while R2 (autonomous) handles planting.

The drive system uses a 4-wheel X-holonomic (omnidirectional) configuration, allowing instantaneous movement in any direction without rotating first — critical for quick repositioning in the arena.

subsystems
subsystems.log
DRIVE BASE
X-holonomic with 4 omni wheels. STM32 handles motor PWM via H-bridge. ROS2 twist commands from RPi4.
SEEDLING PICKER
6-gripper array powered by BLDC motors. Brushless for high torque-to-weight. Each gripper independently actuated.
BALL LAUNCHER
Dual flywheel mechanism with Smart Automation DC motors. Conveyor belt feeds balls into flywheel nip.
FIRMWARE
STM32 handles low-level motor control and encoder feedback. RPi4 runs ROS2 nodes for high-level command interpretation.
POWER
24V LiPo battery pack with PTC fuses per subsystem. Custom PCB for power distribution and motor driver integration.
result

The robot competed in DD Robocon 2025, achieving All India Rank 22 out of hundreds of college teams nationally. This was Team Robomanipal's best result in recent years.
